Superfund Sites

As someone who loves the outdoors, protecting the environment has always been important to me. As such, I was surprised to find out that I had a Superfund Site right in my backyard. A superfund site is a highly contaminated and polluted site deemed to be risky to human health by the EPA.

With this map you can look at all ~1700 Superfund Sites in the United States. The data for this page can be found here.
